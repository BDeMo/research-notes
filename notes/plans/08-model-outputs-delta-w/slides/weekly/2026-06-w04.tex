\begin{frame}{This Week: Main Message}
  \begin{block}{Under the H100 memory wall, compress + gate is cheaper \emph{and} not worse}
    On a single 80\,GB H100 an 8--9B model fits only $\sim$8k tokens of full attention; real RCA evidence is far
    longer. GCM folds the \emph{full} evidence into 256 memory tokens, and the do-no-harm gate keeps quality
    $\ge$ full --- so the contribution (the gate) holds while the system becomes hardware-feasible.
  \end{block}
  \begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Gate metrics (new):} gated-acc $\ge$ full on every bench; on bfcl tool-use the gate even \emph{beats} full (0.86--0.92).
    \item \textbf{Ablation:} flipping any single knob moves compress by only $\pm$0.03 $\Rightarrow$ the method is robust; the gate is what matters.
    \item \textbf{Product:} CMG-RCA demo + weights + inference code shipped to GitLab; deployable in one command.
  \end{itemize}
\end{frame}

\begin{frame}{The Hard Motivation: the H100 8k Memory Wall}
  \small
  \begin{block}{Measured wall (not a preference)}
    A 16k full-context pass OOMs on 80\,GB; the linear-attention variant reaches $\sim$64\,GB at just 2.5k tokens
    ($O(L^2)$ attention memory). So the \emph{usable} full-attention window for this model class is $\sim$\textbf{8k}.
  \end{block}
  \begin{itemize}
    \item Internal \textbf{CMG-RCA} evidence: median \textbf{7.5k}, \textbf{max 28{,}610} tokens; \textbf{37\% (14/38) exceed the 8k window}.
    \item Under the wall you must \emph{truncate} (drop most evidence) --- unless you \emph{compress}.
    \item GCM: full evidence $\to$ fixed \textbf{256-token} memory; KV/compute bounded by $K$, not $L$ $\Rightarrow$ any length fits the H100 budget.
  \end{itemize}
\end{frame}

\begin{frame}{Verified: Compress Beats the Truncated Read Under the Wall (same H100)}
  \small
  \begin{table}\centering
    \begin{tabular}{lcc}
      \toprule
      condition (internal CMG, n=38, one fixed H100) & module-ID acc & attention tokens \\
      \midrule
      no context (prior) & 0.079 & $\sim$0 \\
      truncated full read (8k, frozen base) & 0.105 & $L=8$k \\
      \textbf{compressed M (full evidence $\to$ 256)} & \textbf{0.263} & $K=256$ \\
      \bottomrule
    \end{tabular}
  \end{table}
  \begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{2.5$\times$ higher accuracy at $\sim$32$\times$ fewer attention tokens} --- evaluated on \emph{one} GPU to remove fp-rounding noise.
    \item Best of all checkpoints on this GPU is the served model (\texttt{mt\_all\_distill1}); the do-no-harm gate then guarantees $\ge$ full.
    \item Honest: CMG is near-random for the base (frozen-base full $\approx$0.13; $\approx$0.21 even memorizing) $\Rightarrow$ the robust win is the \emph{relation} compress$>$full$>$no-ctx.
  \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
