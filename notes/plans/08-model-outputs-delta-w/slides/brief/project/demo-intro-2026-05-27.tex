\begin{frame}{Demo Introduction: RCA Demo and Long-Context RCA}
  \begin{block}{Goal}
    Show what we have built in `rca-demo`, what the SFT experiments taught us,
    and why the next demo should test long-context RCA compression.
  \end{block}

  \begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{What we built}: an RCA SFT matrix over Qwen2.5 / Qwen3 bases,
      curriculum vs. mix checkpoints, 4 public RCA datasets, and 5 general benchmarks.
    \item \textbf{Demo asset}: `rca-chat` can load matrix checkpoints and sample
      RCA cases to inspect JSON format, evidence use, and qualitative behavior.
    \item \textbf{What we learned}: SFT can teach RCA signals, but direct
      finetuning has format stability, evidence grounding, and retention risks.
    \item \textbf{Next demo}: compare full context, summary, retrieval, and
      compressed-memory RCA on the same public RCA case.
  \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
