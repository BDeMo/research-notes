## Stage 1 тАФ the apparent coupling (later found confounded)
The 41-cell grid showed a **uniformly positive** per-head LC├ЧCF coupling (every long-context metric ~ every forgetting metric, +0.17тАж0.56). Headline at the time: "the long-context heads are the forgetting heads." See `grid-metrics-2026-06-04.md`.

## Stage 2 тАФ the reckoning (controls kill the head-level claim)
Adding the controls a reviewer would demand (partial-correlation out activation magnitude; **within-layer** to remove depth; per-model not pooled; bootstrap CIs; drop the qwen3.5 hybrid artifact) **dissolved the head-level coupling**. Per model it is weak/тЙИ0/negative; the "survivor" `attn_distance~dW_drift` had within-layer ╧БтЙИ0 (a depth effect). The a-priori gradient signal is **null-to-negative**. тЖТ `mechanism-results-2026-06-04.md`. Lesson: cross-cell pooling + activation magnitude + depth + a hybrid model manufactured a phantom coupling.

## Stage 3 тАФ the surviving phenomena (real, controlled)
Across the full-attention ladder (0.6тЖТ14B) + GLM-4 + Qwen2.5, with a-priori GSM8K gradients:
- **F-shield. Long-context structural heads are gradient-SHIELDED.** Within-layer ╧Б(grad, `attn_distance`) = тИТ0.29тАжтИТ0.60 and ╧Б(grad, `sink`) тЙИ тИТ0.05тАжтИТ0.57 in **every** family тЖТ SFT gradients *avoid* long-reach/sink heads. Mediator: gradient тИЭ activation magnitude (╧Б(grad,`out_norm`) +0.35тАж+0.64), and long-context heads are lower-activation; on Qwen3, also low attention-entropy (saturated attention) тЖТ smaller gradient.
- **F-scale. The shielding fades with scale.** Layer-level ╧Б(long-ctx, grad): 0.6тАУ1.7B тЙИ тИТ0.75 тЖТ 8B тЙИ тИТ0.14 тЖТ 14B тЙИ 0. Small models shield hard; large models neutralize.
- **F-consequence.** This explains why **NIAH stays robust under SFT** (every H3 attempt): long-context isn't where forgetting lands.
- **F-collapse (from Stage-0, still holds).** The representation-collapse axis (eff-rankтЖУ/anisotropyтЖС/massive-actsтЖС/prediction-crystallizes) is robust and vendor-independent тАФ but it is *structural*, not the LCтЖФCF bridge we thought.
